Peonies!

The Peony, or Paeonia, is a beloved perennial known for its large, showy blooms and lush foliage, making it a cornerstone in many gardens.Peonies are native to various regions, including Asia, Europe, and Western North America. This genus encompasses approximately 33 species, falling into two primary categories: herbaceous perennials and deciduous shrubs.Peonies carry rich symbolic meanings, often associated with romance, prosperity, and good fortune. They are also known for their medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
