Hydrangeas!

Hydrangea is a genus of flowering plants celebrated for its large, ornamental flower clusters and lush foliage. Known for their ability to change flower color based on soil pH, hydrangeas are prized additions to gardens, landscapes, and floral arrangements worldwide. They belong to the Hydrangeaceae family.
